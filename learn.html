At recycle, we're passionate about environmental preservation and waste management. We've introduced an innovative initiative to incentivize individuals like you to actively participate in recycling efforts. By joining our platform, you can earn rewards while making a positive impact on the environment.

Earn while You Recycle

With our unique system, you can earn 5 shillings for every plastic bottle you recycle through our platform. We believe in rewarding responsible waste management and promoting sustainability.

How it Works

Open the Recycle website application and scan the barcode on your plastic bottle. Our app verifies the barcode's uniqueness to ensure no repetitions. Once validated, you'll receive 5 shillings as a reward.

Seamless Transactions

To make the process even easier, we've integrated with M-Pesa, a popular mobile payment system. Once your barcode scan is verified, the earned amount is swiftly transferred to your M-Pesa account, ensuring you receive your rewards promptly.

Together Towards a Greener Future

Our aim is to raise environmental consciousness, reduce plastic waste, and foster a culture of recycling. By providing an incentive to recycle, we encourage individuals to actively engage in sustainable practices, creating a cleaner and greener environment for everyone.

Importance Of Recycling Plastics

  1. Environmental Preservation
  2. Resource Conservation
  3. Energy Savings
  4. Waste Reduction

At Recycle, we're committed to environmental preservation and community engagement. By participating in our innovative program, you not only earn rewards but also contribute to a sustainable future. Join us in making a difference and embrace the culture of recycling.
